I will speak in the Birds of a Feather session
["Quantum Computing in Supercomputing: A Complex Multimodal Challenge"](https://isc.app.swapcard.com/widget/event/isc-high-performance-2026/planning/UGxhbm5pbmdfNDQwOTI5Mg==)
at [ISC High Performance 2026](https://isc-hpc.com/) in Hamburg.

The session is scheduled for Tuesday, June 23, 2026, from 2:15 PM to 3:15 PM
(Europe/Berlin), in Hall F (2nd Floor), and focuses on practical pathways for
integrating quantum hardware into supercomputing environments.

It covers hardware, software, and algorithmic challenges across the
quantum-classical stack, with emphasis on integration approaches that
supercomputing centers and quantum hardware builders can apply today.

I also have a talk accepted on "Towards Practical Hybrid Quantum Computing: Integrating Hardware, Software, and Applications" in the workshop [Quantum and Hybrid Quantum/Classical Computing Approaches](https://www.hartree.stfc.ac.uk/events/quantum-and-hybrid-quantum-classical-computing-approaches-isc26-workshop/).
