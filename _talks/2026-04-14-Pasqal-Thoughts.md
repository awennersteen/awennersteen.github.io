I moderated the panel "When HPC and Quantum Work Together" at [Pasqal Thoughts 2026](https://www.pasqal.com/events/pasqal-thoughts-2026/) in Paris.

The panel brought together Francesca Gebbia from CINECA, Paco Martin from IBM Quantum, and Félix Givois from GENCI to discuss how HPC centers, quantum vendors, and ecosystem partners are turning quantum systems into usable parts of production computing environments.

The [recording](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JOX29hpeyq0) is available on YouTube.
